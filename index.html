Who we are

About

We are JESO - Junior Enterprise Sønderborg. We are a non-profit organization founded in October 2019 by nine motivated students from SDU Sønderborg. Since October 2020, we have been a part of the JEEurope Network. This means that we are connected to other Junior Enterprises, companies and organizations all over Europe and the world.

About JESO

Our Mission

To empower students in higher education in Denmark to expand their knowledge, encourage entrepreneurship, and help companies grow and improve.

Our Vision

To increase awareness of the Junior Enterprise concept in Denmark and facilitate entry into the business world for students upon graduation.

Our Goal

Equip members with extra knowledge besides the studies and with practical experience to enter the business world after their graduation.We want to become a well-known and acknowledged organization in and around Sønderborg by providing value-creating services to local businesses.

Our Values

We believe in the power of the network. We identify new opportunities and seek new challenges. We prefer actions to words and goals to dreams. We value the internationality and the multiculturalism of our organization.






Our team


JESO currently consists of fourteen motivated students, who are eager to learn, teach each other, and grow this organization by creating value for our clients.
